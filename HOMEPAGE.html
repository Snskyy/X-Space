Solar System
The Solar System is a collection of celestial bodies consisting of a star called the Sun and all objects that are bound by its gravitational force. These objects include eight known planets with elliptical orbits, five dwarf/dwarf planets, 173 identified natural satellites, and millions of other celestial bodies (meteors, asteroids, comets).

Satelite
A satellite is an object in space that orbits or circles around a bigger object. There are two kinds of satellites: natural (such as the moon orbiting the Earth) or artificial (such as the International Space Station orbiting the Earth).
